JEON HYERIN

Architect Student / Seoul

Jeon Hyerin is an architect based in Seoul, Korea. Her practice focuses on the intersection of spatial design, material craft, and urban context. Working across scales from furniture to urban planning, the studio approaches each project as an opportunity to question conventions and discover new spatial possibilities.

The work is characterized by a rigorous attention to detail, a commitment to material honesty, and a belief that architecture should engage meaningfully with its surroundings. Each project begins with careful observation—of site, program, and the lives that will unfold within the spaces created.
